Venetian plaster / polished plaster

Venetian plaster and all the other terms used to describe it are a mix of high quality slaked lime plaster , mixed with marble dust of different sizes depending on the finish required , applied with a spatula or trowel in thin multiple layers which can then be burnished to create a smooth highly polished surface with the illusion of depth and texture or when left un-burnished, Venetian plaster has a matte finish that is rough and stone-like to the touch or can be both visual and textured if different techniques are used. Venetian plasters can be coloured to around 160 different shades and can be highly polished or highly decorative which gives it its great versatility.
