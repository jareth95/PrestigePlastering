At prestige we specialise in all types of rendering from sand and cement renders to lime based colour renders, also we apply synthetic and acrylic based renders . the products are applied to the exterior of the building using slightly different methods and techniques to achieve both a decorative and protective exterior finish Whether it’s a commercial or residential property, a new build or a refurbishment, we have a solution and a system suited to your needs.
Venetian plaster / polished plaster
Venetian plaster and all the other terms used to describe it are a mix of high quality slaked lime plaster , mixed with marble dust of different sizes depending on the finish required , applied with a spatula or trowel in thin multiple layers which can then be burnished to create a smooth highly polished surface with the illusion of depth and texture or when left un-burnished, Venetian plaster has a matte finish that is rough and stone-like to the touch or can be both visual and textured if different techniques are used .
Venetian plasters can be coloured to around 160 different shades and can be highly polished or highly decorative which gives it its great versatility
